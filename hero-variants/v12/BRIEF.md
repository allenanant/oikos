# Variant 12 — Stationery / Letter (evolves v06)

**Direction:** The hero feels like opening a piece of premium stationery — a folded letter from a thoughtful friend, or a Smythson/Papier card. Subtle paper grain, letterpress shadow on type, a single warm seal mark, and a feel of being handed something with care.

**Signature move:**
- Background: cream `#FFF8EB` with a very subtle paper-fibre noise texture (SVG `feTurbulence` + `feColorMatrix` warmed to terracotta, opacity 0.06, multiply blend) — feels like real laid paper
- Centered card composition with generous margins (16% top/bottom, 18% sides):
  - A small foil-stamp-feel monogram top center: `O.` in EB Garamond, terracotta, with a 1px gold underline
  - Below it, the headline in Garamond, with `Oikos.` Roman top, `for a life that` smaller below in Inter italic, then `sustains you.` Garamond italic terracotta
  - All display type carries a subtle letterpress text-shadow: `1px 1px 0 rgba(60,35,15,0.07), -0.5px -0.5px 0 rgba(255,255,255,0.4)` — debossed, not embossed
  - Below the headline: a thin pair of horizontal rules with a tiny hand-feel ornament between them (a fleuron `❦` or a single SVG-drawn olive-leaf in terracotta — beautiful, not cartoon)
  - About paragraph centered, max 52ch, in Inter
  - Two CTAs: primary forest pill, secondary Garamond italic underlined link
- Bottom-corner colophon: `printed in Delhi · MMXXVI` Inter caps, terracotta

**Imagery:** None — the paper is the visual.

**Constraints:**
- Letterpress shadow MUST be subtle (1px max, low opacity)
- Paper grain MUST be subtle (5–8% opacity max)
- The fleuron/ornament: ONE only, not five
- Type stack: ONLY EB Garamond + Inter
- Comforting at a glance: think holding the most thoughtful letter you've received
