# Variant 20 — Curated Objects Flat-lay

**Direction:** A top-down photograph of a curated still-life — a few intentional objects on warm wood: a small terracotta pot with one fern frond, a ceramic mug, a folded linen napkin, maybe a sprig of dried lavender. Soft natural daylight. Type sits beside or below the flat-lay. Like a Kinfolk page spread.

**Signature move:**
- The photograph dominates 60% of the viewport (right or left side), the rest is cream + type
- Composition is quiet and curated: 3–5 objects max, generous breathing space between them, soft top-down lighting, warm wood or cream linen background
- Type composition on the cream side: `Oikos.` Garamond Roman large, `for a life that` Inter italic smaller, `sustains you.` Garamond italic terracotta
- About + CTAs below
- A thin terracotta hairline runs along the boundary between photo and type

**Imagery:** Find Unsplash for `kinfolk flat lay warm wood`, `curated objects still life`, `morning ritual flat lay ceramic`, `linen wood ceramic top down`. Look for warm tones, ONE plant fragment max in the frame, pure daylight.

**Constraints:**
- Maximum ONE plant element in the photograph (a single fern frond or a sprig — NOT a forest of plants)
- Photo must be top-down (flat-lay) — not perspective
- Type stack: ONLY EB Garamond + Inter
- Apply warming filter if cool tones present
- The composition should feel like a moment of morning calm — not a market display
