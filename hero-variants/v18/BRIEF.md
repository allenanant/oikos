# Variant 18 — Atelier Journal Spread

**Direction:** The hero looks like an open spread from an atelier journal — left page has the headline + about copy, right page has a hand-pressed botanical photograph plus a small handwritten-feel annotation in Garamond italic. Like flipping through a designer's working sketchbook.

**Signature move:**
- Two-page spread layout (50/50 with a very subtle center fold shadow):
  - **Left page (cream):** at top, a small Inter eyebrow `JOURNAL · NO. 01 · MAY MMXXVI`. Below, headline composition — `Oikos.` (Garamond Roman large), `for a life that` (smaller, italic), `sustains you.` (Garamond italic terracotta). Below: about paragraph in Inter justified. Below: two CTAs.
  - **Right page (cream, slightly warmer):** a tightly-cropped photograph of a SINGLE pressed/dried botanical (lavender, eucalyptus, fern frond, dried wheat) — like a herbarium specimen photographed on paper. Below the photograph: a small Garamond italic caption like `Field note · pressed Lavandula, Delhi atelier, March '24`. The photograph is bordered with a 1px hairline rectangle as if mounted.
- Center "fold": a very subtle vertical shadow (linear-gradient 1% wide, opacity 0.08) suggesting the spine of an open journal

**Imagery:** Find Unsplash for a single pressed botanical / herbarium specimen on warm paper or fabric. Search: `pressed flower paper`, `dried lavender specimen`, `herbarium press flat lay`. Avoid live foliage scenes.

**Constraints:**
- Both pages on cream backgrounds (not green or terracotta)
- The center fold shadow must be barely perceptible
- Type stack: ONLY EB Garamond + Inter
- The handwritten-feel annotation: Garamond italic — DO NOT use a fake-handwritten script font
- One terracotta accent only (the period in `Oikos.`)
