# Variant 01 — Typographic Manifesto

**Direction:** Pure typography. No photo. No video. The headline IS the visual. Treat the page like a printed manifesto poster — the kind a Kenya Hara or Massimo Vignelli would design.

**Signature move:**
- Oversized EB Garamond italic, breaking across multiple lines with intentional negative space
- One word — "sustains" or "you" — set 2× larger than the rest, anchoring the eye
- Tight kerning, generous leading
- Single decorative botanical fleuron (a small line-art pressed-fern flourish) used once as a punctuation mark, in terracotta

**Inspiration tone:** Kenya Hara's Muji posters, Penguin Classics covers, John Maeda's quiet maximalism

**Constraints:**
- No background photo
- Cream `#FFF8EB` ground, deep forest `#0B3910` ink, terracotta `#B46A2C` accent
- The only image element allowed is the small fleuron (max 80×80 viewport units)
- Whitespace is the design — make 60% of the viewport breathing room
