# Variant 16 — Hotel-Room Interior Fragment

**Direction:** A tightly-cropped photograph of a calm, lived-in interior — a corner of a hotel room or atelier in afternoon light. Maybe a wooden side table with a single plant + book + ceramic cup. Maybe a window with linen curtain and a chair. The photograph is the warmth; the type sits as restrained editorial overlay.

**Signature move:**
- Full-bleed photograph: an interior fragment — wooden surface, soft daylight, ONE plant or natural element + one tactile object. Warm tones, modest composition. Crops you might see in Cereal magazine, Apartamento, Frédéric Magazine.
- Type overlay: bottom-left, in cream Garamond italic on a subtle dark gradient for legibility. Headline + about + CTAs.
- Top-right corner: a small terracotta circle with `2024` and below it `DELHI` tracked Inter caps — a quiet editorial date stamp
- A thin cream rule across the top of the viewport with `OIKOS · BY ANGELINA` masthead Garamond Roman caps centered

**Imagery:** Unsplash search for `quiet interior corner`, `apartment plant table morning`, `linen curtain wooden chair`, `kinfolk interior afternoon light`. Pick warm-toned, ONE plant max in frame, soft natural light.

**Constraints:**
- Photo MUST be a fragment of a room (not a wide scenery shot)
- ONE plant in the photo is fine; multiple plants = too "trees-everywhere"
- Type stack: ONLY EB Garamond + Inter
- Apply warming filter if the photo is cool: `filter: sepia(0.08) saturate(1.05) brightness(1.02)`
- Bottom gradient: `linear-gradient(to top, rgba(60,35,15,0.45) 0%, transparent 50%)`
