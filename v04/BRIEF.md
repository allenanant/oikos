# Variant 04 — Editorial Magazine Cover

**Direction:** Treat the hero as the cover of a quarterly print magazine — Kinfolk, Cereal, Apartamento. Masthead at top. Issue meta. Tightly cropped hero photograph. Cover headline in display Garamond. Quiet sophistication.

**Signature move:**
- Top of viewport: a horizontal masthead bar with `OIKOS` in oversized Garamond Roman caps, below it a tiny Inter line `VOLUME ONE · DELHI · 2024 · BIOPHILIC INTERIOR + CONSCIOUS GIFTING`
- Centered below: a single tightly-cropped photograph of botanical detail — a fern frond, or pressed flowers in linen, or amber jars on rough wood. ~4:5 portrait ratio.
- Below the photo: the cover headline `For a life that sustains you.` in Garamond italic, two lines max
- Bottom thin rule + small caps subline: `the journal of slow rooms — issue 01`
- Optional: a small barcode-style marker at bottom-left, evoking print issue legitimacy

**Inspiration tone:** Cereal Magazine cover, Apartamento, Kinfolk Journal Issue 12, Jordan-era National Geographic

**Constraints:**
- Use a real Unsplash photo (botanical detail, warm tones)
- Strict typographic hierarchy — Garamond ONLY for headlines, Inter ONLY for masthead/meta
- Avoid sans-serif everywhere except meta lines
- Color: cream ground, forest ink, terracotta as ONE accent point
- Should look like it could be printed and held in hand
