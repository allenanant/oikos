# Variant 06 — Letterpress Tactile

**Direction:** Make the page feel printed on real, slightly textured cream paper. Type sits like it was hand-pressed — subtle deboss shadow, slight ink absorption, paper grain. Tactile and old-craft.

**Signature move:**
- Background: cream `#FFF8EB` with a subtle paper-fibre noise SVG texture overlay (8–12% opacity, multiply blend) so the surface feels like real watercolor or laid paper
- All display type uses a CSS `text-shadow` pair: `1px 1px 0 rgba(60,35,15,0.08), -0.5px -0.5px 0 rgba(255,255,255,0.5)` — gives a debossed letterpress look without being fake
- A hairline ornamental rule (Garamond fleuron `❦` or a thin SVG floral curl) above the headline as a printer's mark, terracotta
- Headline: `Oikos.` in caps Garamond on its own line, then `for a life that` smaller, then `sustains you.` Garamond italic, terracotta
- Below: a tiny "PRINTED IN DELHI · MMXXIV" caps mark, mimicking a colophon

**Inspiration tone:** Hatch Show Print, William Morris Kelmscott Press, Penguin Hardback editions

**Constraints:**
- The paper texture must be SUBTLE — too much = dirty
- Letterpress shadows must be a tasteful 1–2px max — heavy = fake
- No photographs
- One ornamental flourish only — not five
