FOR THE READER WHO LINGERS

On the word oíkos.

By Angelina · 4 minutes read
Issue No. 01 · MMXXVI
001 / 014

Long before the word came to mean a balance sheet, or a study of frogs in a pond, οἶκος meant something simpler — the small, breathing world inside a doorway. We've been writing toward that meaning for two years.

οἶκος
oíkos.
noun · ancient greek
home·household·the small ecosystem within it
Centuries later, the same root branched into two words that began their lives as one ↓
A return
to that idea.

Oikos by Angelina is a return to that idea: bringing nature, intention, and lightness into the places we spend our lives.

Our mission is to guide you in creating a thoughtful and lasting change — whether through mindful corporate gifting or biophilic home design.

We believe sustainability isn't just about reducing harm; it's about designing systems that nurture both people and the planet.

Let's collaborate to build a future that sustains us all.

— A.

Biophilic Interiors

Plants chosen

We design offices that draw from nature rather than shut it out — air, texture, and greenery treated as part of the architecture, not decoration laid on top.

Editor's note.

On Intention,
Over Perfection

At Oikos, sustainability isn't about perfection; it's about intention.

We help you bring purpose, creativity, and authenticity into the way you live and work — one conscious choice at a time. The rest follows.

Pillar I.Creative Solutions, Rooted in Nature.

We believe that responsibility can be beautiful.

Our approach blends creativity with ecology — transforming spaces with biophilic art, upcycled materials, and nature-inspired design. Beauty isn't an afterthought to sustainability; it's how sustainability becomes wanted, kept, and lived with.

Pillar II.Practical Guidance, Personal Approach.

We listen, collaborate, and co-create realistic strategies tailored to your lifestyle or business.

Whether it's planning a festive hamper, greening an office, or rethinking how your workplace stocks its essentials, our goal is to make sustainability intuitive — not intimidating. The best sustainable choice is the one a person will actually make twice.

— Angelina · Oikos · Delhi
