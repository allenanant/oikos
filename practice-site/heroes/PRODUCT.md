# Oikos by Angelina — Practice Site Hero

## Who
A small Delhi-based studio for biophilic interiors, conscious gifting, and quieter workplaces. Founded by Angelina. Practice-led, not product-led. Reference site (structure): https://blurringboundaries.in/

## Audience
- Homeowners in Delhi/NCR commissioning a single intimate space (one living room, one nursery).
- Couples ordering small-batch wedding favours (10–40 boxes, not 500).
- Small studio owners and partners-in-firms who want a calmer workplace.

They are 30–55, educated, will type the URL after a friend mentioned the studio. They are not looking for a "service tier". They are looking to see if they like the practice's hand.

## The job of the hero
Make a stranger feel — in the first 3 seconds — that this is a real practice run by a real person, working slowly, with biophilic materials, by hand. Tone: calm, grown-up, matter-of-fact. **Not** lyrical. **Not** corporate. **Not** magazine-glossy. **Not** techy.

## Type
- **Display**: EB Garamond italic (Google Fonts) — for emotional / hero phrases
- **Display alt / utility**: Inter (substitute for paid Alfabet)
- **Body**: Poppins
- Use EB Garamond italic *sparingly* on the key emotional moment. Don't paint the whole hero in italics.

## Anti-slop guards
- **No** purple gradients, neon greens, generic "eco" green badges.
- **No** emoji as icons.
- **No** Inter/Roboto as the display face.
- **No** stock "diverse hands holding plants" photos.
- **No** CSS-drawn leaf SVGs.
- **No** glass morphism, no neumorphism, no glowing borders, no faux-3D.
- **No** plate captions ("Plate I"), drop caps, marginalia, wax seals, postmarks, ASCII art, radars, telemetry tickers, terminal styling, brutalist, HUD, cyberpunk, magazine spread, hand-written letter, field journal, apothecary.
- **No** "elevate / unlock / transform / curate / craft / journey / experience" copy clichés.
- **No** lyrical flourishes like "for a life that steadies you".

## Voice
- Lowercase preferred for section labels and the wordmark.
- Matter-of-fact, practice-led.
- Allowed words: practice, studio, slow, by hand, biophilic, conscious, Delhi, plants, materials, makers, paragraph-essay copy.
- Disallowed: any of the cliché copy verbs above.
