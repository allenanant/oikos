# Variant 17 — Hand-Pressed Seal + Note Card

**Direction:** A center-stage composition like a handwritten, wax-sealed note. An oversized terracotta wax seal (rendered in CSS/SVG with care — texture, irregular edges, a tiny olive sprig pressed into the wax) anchors the layout. Below, the headline as if hand-set on a note card.

**Signature move:**
- Centered vertically, on cream background
- At top: a beautifully-rendered wax seal SVG — circular, terracotta `#B46A2C` to deeper `#6E1919` radial gradient (mimicking real wax depth), irregular wax-drip edges (use SVG `filter: feTurbulence` for organic distortion), inside the seal an `O.` Garamond italic in cream-deep, a tiny pressed olive leaf to the side. Diameter ~140px desktop, 90px mobile.
- Below the seal, a thin terracotta hairline + the headline `For a life that sustains you.` in Garamond italic forest, on its own line
- Above it: `Oikos.` in Garamond Roman, larger
- Below the headline: about copy in Inter, two CTAs
- Bottom: a very small `n. household · ecosystem · home` colophon in Inter italic

**Imagery:** None besides the SVG seal — the seal IS the visual signature.

**Constraints:**
- The wax seal MUST look real and tactile — not a flat circle. Use:
  - Radial gradient for wax depth
  - Subtle inner shadow (`filter: drop-shadow`)
  - Wavy/irregular edge using SVG `feTurbulence` displacement
  - The `O.` inside should look pressed/recessed (subtle dark inner shadow)
- The olive leaf pressed in the wax: tiny, hand-drawn line art SVG, deep forest green, accurate proportions (research olive leaf shape — long oval, opposite paired)
- Type stack: ONLY EB Garamond + Inter
- One single warm point — the seal — surrounded by quiet cream
